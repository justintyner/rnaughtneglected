Our Mission

Wounds Worms and Vectors is dedicated to raising awareness about neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) and supporting the researchers who work tirelessly to understand and combat these conditions. Through our podcast, we aim to bridge the gap between scientific research and public understanding.

What We Cover

Each episode explores different aspects of NTDs, from cutting-edge research and treatment developments to the human stories behind the science. We interview researchers, healthcare workers, and affected communities to provide a comprehensive view of these global health challenges.

Our Vision

We're building a future non-profit organization focused on supporting NTD research and providing resources to researchers worldwide. This podcast is just the beginning of our journey to make a meaningful impact in global health.
